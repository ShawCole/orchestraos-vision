// The Problem

AI tools are everywhere.
AI systems are not.

Everyone has access to ChatGPT. Nobody has 28 agents that know their codebase, their clients, their roadmaps, and can ship production work autonomously overnight.

⚠

Prompt-and-Pray

Most teams use AI as a fancy autocomplete. One prompt, one response, zero persistence. The context vanishes the moment you close the tab.

⌛

Manual Handoffs

You brief a VA. They brief a designer. They brief a developer. Each handoff leaks context and burns hours. AI should eliminate this chain, not add another link.

🚫

No Orchestration

Running Claude in a terminal is not a system. There's no coordination between agents, no memory, no project awareness, no way to manage 10 projects at once.
